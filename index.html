Historically, our club is known on a greater scale as "Cal Animage Mu," originating from our affiliation with the network of collegiate anime clubs called "Cal Animage".
Starting with Cal Animage Alpha (UC Berkley, circa 1989), this network loosely ties together many schools over the Pacific Ocean with a predominance in the U.S. state of California,
whose students have an affinity for Japanese animation and culture, serving these collegiate communities by bringing together unique
media and individuals unlike any other. With the "Mu" meaning the 12th of the 15 members, TAC appreciates this rich history and has vowed to maintain its legacy since 1993!

With all that said, please join us as we are open to all who love anime and enjoy its fanfare, wherein we strive for inclusivity and anti-bias amongst our members.
