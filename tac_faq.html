    • For Thursdays, we do a more general meeting where we pick a specific theme for the week (ex. idol, action, horror) and have our members pitch anime based on that theme. We divide the pool in two categories based on years, with one category covering recent anime and the other category covering everything before that. We then choose two anime (one from each category) by popular vote, and, in addition to those two anime, officers will show a third "feature" anime of their choosing.
